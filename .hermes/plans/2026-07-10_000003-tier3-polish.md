## Task 2: Version Snapshots

**What:** Every time a chapter is saved, keep the previous version. User can browse and restore old versions.

**Files:**
- Create: `lib/domain/manuscript/chapter_snapshot.dart` — snapshot domain model
- Create: `lib/data/database/tables/chapter_snapshot_table.dart`
- Create: `lib/data/dao/snapshot_dao.dart`
- Create: `lib/presentation/features/manuscript/widget/snapshot_history_sheet.dart`
- Modify: `lib/presentation/features/manuscript/screen/manuscript_screen.dart`

**Flow:** SaveContent → create Snapshot(prevContent, timestamp). History button in editor → bottom sheet listing snapshots → tap to preview → "Restore" button.
